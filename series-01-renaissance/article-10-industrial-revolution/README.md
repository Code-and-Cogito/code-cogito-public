# Article 10 — Industrial Revolution Analysis

**The Industrial Revolution: When Machines Changed Everything**

Using Python to visualize the GDP hockey stick, urbanization surge, and the human cost of industrialization.

## Quick Start

```bash
pip install numpy matplotlib
python industrial_revolution_analysis.py
```

## What You'll Get

**Terminal output:**
- UK GDP growth 1750-1900
- Urbanization shift percentages
- Life expectancy drop during peak industrialization

**Charts:**
- `gdp_hockey_stick.png` — The famous hockey stick: GDP per capita 1700-1900
- `urbanization.png` — Rural to urban population shift over 150 years
- `working_conditions.png` — Life expectancy, working hours, and child labor rates

## Key Findings

- UK GDP grew dramatically from 1750 to 1900
- Urbanization surged from minority to majority urban population
- Life expectancy initially **dropped** during peak industrialization before recovering
- The "price of progress" was paid disproportionately by the working class
