# Article 11 — Darwin Analysis

**Darwin's Evolution: The Final Revolution in Human Status**

Using Python to simulate natural selection, visualize Darwin's impact across disciplines, and trace the timeline from the Beagle voyage to the publication of *On the Origin of Species*.

## Quick Start

```bash
pip install numpy matplotlib
python darwin_analysis.py
```

## What You'll Get

**Terminal output:**
- Years from Beagle voyage to publication (28 years)
- Trait shift after simulated generations of selection
- Theology certainty drop (-58%)

**Charts:**
- `natural_selection_sim.png` — Natural selection simulation: trait distribution shifts over generations
- `darwin_impact_radar.png` — Darwin's impact across biology, theology, philosophy, sociology, and psychology
- `darwin_timeline.png` — Key events from the Beagle voyage (1831) to the Oxford debate (1860)

## Key Findings

- Darwin waited **28 years** between the Beagle voyage and publishing — why?
- Natural selection simulation shows measurable trait shifts in just 50 generations
- Theology's certainty score dropped from 95 to 40 (-58%) after Darwin
- The impact extended far beyond biology into philosophy, psychology, and sociology
